Running

In my spare time I like to run with a great team called Students Run CHicago

I have been running since my junior year of highschool, when I did my first half marathon with CARA students, it was then that the program closed and SRC(Students Run Chicago) came to be. I actually ran with the people who created SRC and was invited to continue running with them and proceed to run a marathon. At first I was very scared.... because its a MARATHON!

Later that year when I was back in school, I decided to join the track team and went on to feel amazing during EVERY run.. even the long ones! so I decided that would be the year I would run it.
